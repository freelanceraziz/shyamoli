To meet the need of technical hands in home and abroad Shyamoli Ideal Polytechnic Institute has been established in 1979 under Bangladesh Technical Education Board. Principal M.A. Sattar is the founder if this reputed technical instiution. It has four campuses located at Dhaka, Chittagang, Rangpur, Lakshmipur. It is a pioneer polytechnic institution in Bangladesh.



SIPI has a placement Center. This Center provides career development counseling to SIPI students. Cultivates and maintains relationship with empolyers, relevant institutiions and organization. Designs and arrange seminars, workshops related to development of students.
